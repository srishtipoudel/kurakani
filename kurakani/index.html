We are here to hear you !

Mental health has been a major concern of people in this technology era. People find hard to share thier feelings with thier friends and sometime even feels more anxious and exhausted. Keeping that thing in mind team OldPine created this website as a sample website for KuHackfest 2021. Kurakani is a responsive website for encouraging people of any age to talk about their mind. This website contains features like chatbot for emergency response which has features like asking tips and entertainment, calling doctors being anonymus, mental health related blogs and creating a circle of their own kind of people.

Why metal health?

Our Motivation

Close to 800 000 people die due to suicide every year, which is one person every 40 seconds. Suicide is a global phenomenon and occurs throughout the lifespan. Effective and evidence-based interventions can be implemented at population, sub-population and individual levels to prevent suicide and suicide attempts. There are indications that for each adult who died by suicide there may have been more than 20 others attempting suicide. Our society just see the people having mental health issue as different way, even while meeting pshychiatrist. We through this platform wanna reduce the gap between the people having mental health issues and the closeness they get. Feel free to ride with us.

Communities

We always want best for you people, who needed to be heard. So for the people who wanna join communities and find people of same interest and thinking have few communities. And for those who wanna change their thinking into writings or songs, we do have communities for such people too. Just be cool and your heart decide the vibe now ! Being introvert can be good sometime. And make sure if you feel insecure sharing your thoughts, we accept anonymus creations too !
